# Homepage — Section 07

# Membership

> Membership should feel like an enhancement, never a requirement.

---

# Purpose

After users understand Vivah,

trust the platform,

and imagine themselves using it,

they naturally ask

"What do I receive as a member?"

This section answers that question without creating pressure.

Membership should feel optional.

The relationship always comes first.

---

# Emotional Journey

```
Confidence

↓

Understanding

↓

Choice

↓

Commitment
```

---

# Philosophy

Most subscription pages sell fear.

Unlimited Likes.

Boost Yourself.

Don't Miss Out.

Vivah refuses this approach.

Membership exists to improve the experience.

Not manipulate behaviour.

---

# Core Principles

Membership should communicate

Value.

Transparency.

Respect.

Choice.

Never

Urgency.

Scarcity.

Pressure.

Artificial limitations.

---

# Section Structure

```
Headline

↓

Supporting Copy

↓

Comparison Cards

↓

Membership Benefits

↓

Primary CTA
```

---

# Headline

Maximum

Eight words.

Examples

Membership That Respects Your Journey.

Choose The Experience That Fits You.

Designed Around Meaningful Connections.

---

# Supporting Copy

Maximum

Three lines.

Explain

Every person can begin their journey.

Membership simply provides additional convenience and support.

---

# Membership Cards

Display

Three plans.

Basic

↓

Premium

↓

Elite

Each card receives equal visual importance.

Only the recommended plan receives subtle emphasis.

Never dominate the page.

---

# Card Contents

Plan Name.

Short Description.

Monthly Price.

Yearly Price (optional).

Included Features.

CTA.

Maximum

Six benefits.

---

# Recommended Plan

Highlight using

Slight elevation.

Subtle border.

Small badge.

Never use

Flashing labels.

Animated ribbons.

Large gradients.

---

# Pricing Language

Be transparent.

Never

Hidden charges.

Confusing conditions.

Tiny disclaimers.

Explain

Exactly what users receive.

---

# Comparison Design

Users should compare comfortably.

Whitespace dominates.

Typography remains clear.

Icons remain minimal.

No dense pricing tables.

---

# CTA

Examples

Choose Your Membership.

Compare Plans.

Continue With Basic.

Every option should feel equally respected.

---

# Motion

Allowed

Soft reveal.

Card elevation.

Small hover.

Forbidden

Pulsing buttons.

Animated pricing.

Countdowns.

Confetti.

---

# Accessibility

Pricing should remain readable.

Support keyboard navigation.

Maintain semantic headings.

High contrast.

Touch targets.

---

# Performance

Static content.

Minimal JavaScript.

SVG icons.

Optimized rendering.

---

# AI Implementation Rules

Always

Respect free users.

Present pricing honestly.

Maintain visual balance.

Consume Design Tokens.

Never

Create fake urgency.

Display countdowns.

Use manipulative copy.

Hide pricing.

---

# Review Checklist

□ Is pricing transparent?

□ Are all plans respected?

□ Is the recommended plan subtle?

□ Is there no urgency?

□ Is accessibility complete?

□ Does the section feel trustworthy?

Only if every answer is "Yes" should this section be approved.
