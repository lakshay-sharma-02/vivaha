The Homepage Footer is not another navigation menu.

It is the final moment of reassurance before visitors leave the page.

Visitors should leave with complete confidence that Vivah is

Professional.

Trustworthy.

Accessible.

Transparent.

Human.

"What if I still need help?"

Everything inside the Footer exists to answer that question.
