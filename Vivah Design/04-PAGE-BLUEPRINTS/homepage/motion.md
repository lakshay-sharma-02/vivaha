# Homepage Motion Blueprint

> Motion should make the homepage feel alive, never animated.

---

# Purpose

Motion exists to guide attention.

Not attract it.

Visitors should remember the atmosphere.

Never the animations.

Every movement should reduce cognitive effort.

Never increase it.

---

# Motion Philosophy

Imagine standing inside a peaceful modern home.

Curtains move slightly.

Leaves sway gently.

Morning light slowly changes.

Nothing demands attention.

Everything feels alive.

That is Vivah's motion language.

---

# Environmental Motion

Allowed

Moving sunlight.

Subtle botanical movement.

Soft ambient gradients.

Tiny floating dust particles.

Very slow background movement.

Duration

8–20 seconds.

Users should barely notice it.

---

# Section Reveal

Each homepage section reveals only once.

Preferred animation

Fade

+

16–24px upward movement.

Duration

300–450ms.

Delay

40–80ms between grouped elements.

Never stagger dozens of elements.
