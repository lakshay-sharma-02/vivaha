# Philosophy

Vivah should never brag.

It should celebrate people.

This section is not about Vivah's success.

It is about the beginning of real relationships.

Vivah is simply the bridge.

---

# Core Principles

Every story should feel

Authentic.

Respectful.

Personal.

Hopeful.

Never

Perfect.

Scripted.

Promotional.

Overly emotional.

# Headline

Maximum

Eight words.

Examples

Real Stories.

---

# Supporting Copy

Maximum

Three lines.

Explain

Every meaningful relationship begins with a single conversation.

Vivah simply creates that opportunity.

---

# Featured Story

One story receives editorial emphasis.

Contains

Couple photograph.

Names.

City.

Journey summary.

Short quotation.

Never exceed

120 words.

---

# Additional Stories

Display

Three stories.

Desktop

Three-column layout.

Tablet

Two-column layout.

Mobile

Vertical stack.

Every story contains

Photo.

Names.

Location.

40-word summary.

---

# Photography

Natural.

Real.

Everyday moments.

Home.

Garden.

Walking together.

Family gatherings.

Avoid

Wedding stage photography.

Luxury venues.

Over-edited portraits.

Stock imagery.

---

# Quotations

Keep quotations short.

Examples

"We found someone who truly understood our family."

"It felt respectful from the very beginning."

"Our conversations felt natural."

Maximum

Twenty-five words.

---

# Story Design

Each story should prioritize

People

↓

Experience

↓

Emotion

Never

Platform.

Technology.

Marketing.

---

# Motion

Allowed

Soft fade.

Card reveal.

Image transition.

Forbidden

Carousels.

Auto sliding.

Auto play.

Animated quotes.

---

# CTA

Examples

Read More Stories.

Explore Success Stories.

Begin Your Own Journey.

---

# Accessibility

Photographs require descriptive alt text.

Stories must remain readable without images.

Quotes should never exist as images.

---

# Performance

Responsive photography.

Lazy loading.

Compressed media.

Skeleton placeholders.

No autoplay videos.

---

# AI Implementation Rules

Always

Use authentic photography.

Keep stories concise.

Highlight emotion.

Never

Invent unrealistic testimonials.

Use exaggerated claims.

Display fake review scores.

Use stock marketing language.

---

# Review Checklist

□ Do the stories feel authentic?

□ Are people the focus?

□ Is every story believable?

□ Is hope communicated naturally?

□ Does the section avoid marketing language?

□ Is accessibility complete?

Only if every answer is "Yes" should this section be approved.
