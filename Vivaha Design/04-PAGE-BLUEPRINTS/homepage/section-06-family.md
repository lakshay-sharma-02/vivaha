# Homepage — Section 06

# Family Experience

> Marriage is rarely an individual journey. Vivaha respectfully acknowledges the role of families without taking away individual choice.

---

# Purpose

This section differentiates Vivaha from dating platforms.

It communicates that relationships are built not only between two people,

but often between two families.

The experience should feel inclusive,

never controlling.

# Philosophy

Families are participants.
