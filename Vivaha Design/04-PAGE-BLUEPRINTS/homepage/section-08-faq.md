# Homepage — Section 08

# Frequently Asked Questions

> Questions are signs of interest. Good answers remove hesitation before it becomes doubt.

---

# Purpose

The FAQ is the final opportunity to remove uncertainty.

It should answer the questions users are most likely to have before creating an account.

Not every possible question.

Only the important ones.

---

# Emotional Journey

```
Confidence

↓

Clarity

↓

Reassurance

↓

Readiness
```

---

# Philosophy

Good FAQs reduce support requests.

Great FAQs increase trust.

Every answer should sound like a thoughtful conversation.

Never like legal documentation.

---

# Recommended Questions

Exactly eight.

---

## Is Vivaha free to join?

Explain Basic membership clearly.

---

## How are profiles verified?

Explain verification simply.

---

## Can my family participate?

Explain family involvement respectfully.

---

## Is my personal information private?

Explain privacy controls.

---

## How does Vivaha recommend profiles?

Discuss compatibility in simple language.

Do not mention algorithms.

---

## Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Answer transparently.

---

## How do conversations begin?

Explain mutual interest and respectful communication.

---

## How can I contact support?

Provide clear support information.

# AI Implementation Rules

Always

Write concise answers.

Maintain a reassuring tone.

Support accessibility.

Consume Design Tokens.

Never

Invent questions.

Use marketing language.

Avoid answering difficult topics.

Hide important policies.

---

# Review Checklist

□ Are only important questions included?

□ Are answers clear?

□ Is the tone reassuring?

□ Is the accordion accessible?

□ Does the section reduce hesitation?

□ Would first-time visitors feel ready afterwards?

Only if every answer is "Yes" should this section be approved.
