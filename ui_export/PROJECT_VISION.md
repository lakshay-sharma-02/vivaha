# Project Vision: Vivaha

## Core Concept
Vivaha is a premium, highly-vetted, traditional matchmaking platform for serious individuals and families. 
It is NOT a swipe-based casual dating app (like Tinder or Bumble), and it is NOT an ad-filled, generic legacy matrimony spreadsheet (like BharatMatrimony or Shaadi.com).

## Key Differentiators
1. **Manual Verification:** Every profile undergoes human verification (Aadhaar, Phone number) to ensure trust and eliminate fake profiles.
2. **Premium Subscription:** ₹5,000 for 30 Days. This high barrier to entry filters out non-serious users and creates a highly intent-driven community.
3. **Family & Individual Balanced:** Captures important traditional data (Gotra, Family Type, Caste) but presents it in a respectful, modern, and individual-first storytelling format.

## Desired UI/UX Aesthetics (For the Redesign)
- **Vibe:** "Exclusive Private Members' Club" or "High-End Editorial Boutique".
- **Color Palette:** Warm, trust-inspiring colors. Ivory/cream backgrounds, subtle gold/bronze accents, deep maroon, navy, or forest green for primary elements.
- **Typography:** Sophisticated serif fonts for headings (like Playfair Display, Merriweather) to convey tradition and elegance, paired with highly legible sans-serif for body text.
- **Layout:** Generous whitespace, editorial-style presentation of profiles (clean cards separating Bio, Family, Lifestyle). 
- **The "Anti-Swipe":** Curated, high-quality matches rather than infinite scrolling. Emphasize verification badges prominently.

## Architecture Context
- Built on **Next.js (App Router)** with **Tailwind CSS**.
- Authentication and Database powered by **Supabase**.
- Components built with **shadcn/ui**.
- We use `next/image` for optimized imagery and `lucide-react` for iconography.
